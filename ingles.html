Sustainable English Description
Profesora: Maria Eugenia Lopez Ruiz

Sustainable development (also called sustainable development or sustainable development)
is a model of societal development that seeks to reconcile economic and industrial needs with social and ecological or environmental balance.
Its goal is that economic development does not threaten life on the planet,
so that it can be preserved for future generations.

It is a type of development that seeks a balance between three main pillars: economic development, care for the environment and the social development of populations.
This balance is known as sustainability and, to achieve this, it seeks to carry out practices such as the responsible and efficient use of resources,
the study of the environmental impact of some practices and the search for new forms, applications or technologies.
Companies and organizations around the world aim to achieve sustainable development that allows them to meet their economic objective,
but without causing harm to the natural or social environment.
